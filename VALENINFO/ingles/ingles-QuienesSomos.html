Who Are We?

We are a passionate community of computer enthusiasts in Valencia, aiming to connect enthusiasts and professionals in the field. We gather to share knowledge, learn from others, and enjoy group activities.

What Do We Do?

We organize events such as hackathons, programming workshops, and mentoring sessions on topics like web development, artificial intelligence, and system maintenance. Our goal is to create a space where everyone can improve their technical skills.

Our Goal

Our mission is to foster collaborative learning and strengthen the technology ecosystem in Valencia. We seek to connect individuals from various levels of experience, creating opportunities to develop projects and establish professional networks.
