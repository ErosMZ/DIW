Logo Design

The ValenInfo logo is designed to convey professionalism and creativity, with a focus on computing and innovation.

  • Circular Shape: Represents unity and collaboration among association members.
  • Gear: Symbolizes the technical work and mechanics behind computing.
  • Lightbulb: A clear reference to creativity and innovative ideas that arise from our projects.

The white and black colors provide an elegant and professional contrast, while the bold text highlights the association's identity. This minimalist design ensures the logo is easily recognizable and adaptable to different formats and platforms.

Fonts and Colors

I use Arial and Roboto fonts for their modern and readable style. These fonts are ideal for ensuring text clarity.

The main colors are:

  • Dark blue: used in titles and highlighted elements.
  • Light blue: applied to buttons and links to convey trust.
  • White: used as the main background to improve contrast.
  • Gray: used in text to avoid pure black and soften reading.

Responsive Design

The page is designed with Flexbox and Grid to ensure that elements adapt to different screens, from mobile to desktop.

I use @media to adjust navigation and content on small devices.

Interactivity

Buttons and links have hover effects, such as color changes and smooth 0.3s transitions. This improves the user experience.

The logo has a @keyframes animation, smoothly rotating to add a dynamic touch.

Fonts and CSS Styles

On this page, I have used two main fonts to ensure a modern and readable appearance on all devices. The fonts are:

  • Arial: Used in most parts of the page for common text and forms. It is a very common sans-serif font and easy to read on screens.
  • Roboto: Used in the contact section. It is a sans-serif font with a more modern and stylish look.

These fonts are implemented in the font-family property of CSS:


font-family: Arial, sans-serif;
font-family: 'Roboto', Arial, sans-serif;

Additionally, I have used various CSS classes to style elements such as buttons and forms:

  • Buttons: Buttons change color when the cursor hovers over them, achieved with the hover property.

  • Forms: Forms are designed with a light background, rounded borders, and shadows to make them visually appealing and easy to use.

Rounded Borders

Rounded borders are applied using the border-radius property in CSS. This allows elements like buttons, boxes, and forms to have soft corners, improving the aesthetics and user experience.

Example: border-radius: 10px;
